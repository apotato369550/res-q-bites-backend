Here are the relationships shown in the ERD:

A user can be an individual donor, an LGU user, an establishment, or an admin.
A user can give zero or many donations; each donation belongs to exactly one user.
A user can view zero or many donation history records; each donation history record is viewed by exactly one user.
A user can receive zero or many notifications; each notification belongs to exactly one user.

An LGU can administer zero or many users; a user may be assigned to zero or one LGU.
An LGU can manage zero or many beneficiaries; each beneficiary belongs to exactly one LGU.
An LGU can stock zero or many inventory items; each inventory item belongs to exactly one LGU.
An LGU can perform zero or many distributions; each distribution belongs to exactly one LGU.
An LGU can be assigned zero or many donations; each donation may be assigned to zero or one LGU.

A donation can become zero or many inventory items; each inventory item may come from zero or one donation.
A donation can log zero or many donation history records; each donation history record belongs to exactly one donation.

A beneficiary can receive zero or many distributions; each distribution belongs to exactly one beneficiary.

An inventory item can be used in zero or many distributions; each distribution uses exactly one inventory item.
