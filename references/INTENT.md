For today's scrum update, I will be presenting our project, ResQ Bites: Saving Food, Feeding Hope, a mobile-based platform that helps reduce food waste and address food insecurity in Cebu City.

The system allows restaurants, establishments, and individuals to donate surplus food through the app. Instead of direct pickup, donations are received and managed by LGUs or barangays, which act as community food banks and distribute the food to qualified beneficiaries.

Key features include food donation posting, LGU donation management, inventory tracking, analytics, and a gamification system where donors earn points and rewards for successful donations.

For this sprint, we have completed the requirements gathering and system analysis phases. We are currently working on the user interface, database design, and donation workflow. Our next step is prototype development and testing.

Through technology and community partnership, ResQ Bites aims to turn food waste into hope for those in need.
